<p>One man's protest at the airport's restriction on carrying electronic devices onboard resulted in him ironically proving the policy rightThe Istanbul Alat&uuml;rk Airport said an unnamed passenger flung his powerbank on the ground in anger, after he was told that he couldn't bring it onboard his flight to the UK.</p>
<p>The man reportedly got into an argument with security officials at the airport, and the battery pack ignited after it crashed on the ground, Turkey's&nbsp;<a href="http://www.dha.com.tr/6/ataturk-havalimaninda-panik-yaratan-patlama/haber-1536000" target="_blank">Dogan news agency</a>&nbsp;reported.</p>
