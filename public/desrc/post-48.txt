<p><strong>A fire that broke out&nbsp;</strong><strong>on</strong><strong>&nbsp;the ground floor of Bangladesh Railway building in Dhaka was doused this morning.</strong></p>
<p>The fire originated from an electric short circuit at the generator room around 10:00am, said Md Tanharul Islam, senior station officer.</p>
<p>Two units of firefighters doused it around 10:45am, he added</p>
<p>Meanwhile, Railways Minister Mujibul Haque visited the spot immediately after the incident.</p>
<p>&ldquo;Some mongooses were running over the circuits desperately in the generator room, causing the fire,&rdquo; he told reporters.</p>
<div class="mobile-adv-in-body pull-left pad-left-right-big gero-left pad-bottom-small">&nbsp;</div>
<p>&ldquo;The firefighters have saved the building at the right time before a major fire accident took&nbsp;place,&rdquo; he added.</p>
